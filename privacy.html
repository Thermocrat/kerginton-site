This Privacy Policy explains how Kerington LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website or submit a reservation. By using the site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide on our reservation form: your name, business name, email address, and phone number.
  • Reservation details: the store, package, number of carts, and term you select, plus any notes you add.
  • Basic technical data that most websites receive automatically, such as your browser type and general location, used only to keep the site running and secure.

We do not collect or store payment card numbers on this website. When payment is required, it is handled by a secure third-party payment processor [for example, Stripe], and your card details go directly to that processor, not to us.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your reservation and confirm availability.
  • To prepare and send an invoice and set up your monthly billing.
  • To provide and improve our advertising and design services.
  • To contact you about your order or, if you've agreed, about related offers.

How your information is handled

When you submit the form, your details are delivered to us by email through our form provider [Formspree]. The website itself is hosted by [Cloudflare Pages]. These providers process your information only to deliver their service to us. We do not sell your personal information.

Who we share it with

We share information only with service providers who help us run the business, such as our form/email provider, website host, and (when you pay) our payment processor. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data retention

We keep reservation and contact information for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal or accounting requirements, then delete it.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop contacting you, by emailing us at the address below. We'll respond within a reasonable time.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy
